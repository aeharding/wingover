Sync, if you want it

Off by default: your location never leaves your phone unless you turn sync on, and then only to the server you chose. Subscribe to back up flights and open them anywhere, or point Wingover at your own server for free.

Simple on purpose

No feeds, no leaderboards, no locked features, no forty-page settings. Wingover records, logs, and plans, and refuses to do more. No account, nothing to configure, one tap to fly. Open source, so it stays that way.
